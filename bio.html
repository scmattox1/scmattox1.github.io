Me

Hello! My name is Sarah. Welcome to my portfolio site! I am currently working as a Web Designer in South Bend, Indiana. I am willing to work anywhere in the West Michigan or Michiana area.

My Work...

I greatly enjoy new creative and challenging projects. I have been told many times that I treat the sites I work on 'like they are my babies.' Not only do I care about what I am working on, but I want to be involve in its creation, its development, and what happens to it after I am done.

In the past few years, I have created and implemented site redeisgns and responsive layouts. While working on these redesigns, I incorporated the usage of SVGs, font icons, and fluid CSS layouts using primarily flebox. I also worked on maintaing multi-brand consistency and creating living style guidelines for other designers to reference.

I have a moderate understanding of SCSS, JQuery, Java, and other back-end development languages. When applicable and time-allowed, I work on these skills within my work.

My career has been mostly in computer art, web design, and front-end development. I have a school background in computer animation which was focused primarily on character rigging, modeling, and animation. I also have studied art in various medias.
